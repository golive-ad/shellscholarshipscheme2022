Shell Scholarship
For JAMB Candidates 2022/2023

The Shell Petroleum Development Company(SPDC) alongside Shell Nigeria Production and Exploration Company (SNPECo) has offered free scholarships to 1,000 jamb candidates.
All JAMB candidates are required to apply immediately irrespective of JAMB score.

The requirements for application are as follows: JAMB score must be up to 160, Full name used in registation, JAMB Reg. no. and any working email address for contact.
Emails should be checked regularly for further information.
